Upholstery services

We offer upholstery services for any project big or small, whether you’re working with a designer already or would like our expertise in coordinating fabrics for a project. We also ship Fabric, Cushions, Window Coverings, and Furniture throughout the United States.

About Frama

Frama Exclusive's founder, Maritza Bermudez, has been in the upholstery business for over 20 years. Her artistic mastery and passion for the trade, show in all of her pieces. Her knowledge of the trade is a rare find!

  • Family Owned

    Family owned and operated in Wilton, CT since 1995. Frama Exclusives specializes in all things upholstered, custom built furniture and commercial seating.

  • Client satisfaction

    Our personalized approach and result oriented service has grown the once small work room, into a thriving business. We cater to clients who appreciate detail and want the design process to be a collaborative, educational, and above all an enjoyable experience.

  • Acknowledgments

    We’ve been lucky enough to have our work featured in several magazines including Country Living, Westport Magazine, and Connecticut Cottage.
